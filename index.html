Grenoble

Location.


Map by google maps.

Grenoble is located in the south-east of france, at the base of the Alps. Due to its size, location and importance, Grenoble is often known as "The capital of the Alps"

The Alps

The Alps are the largest mountain range in Europe, including many of the tallest mountains in the world. The tallest and highest mountain of the Alps, Mont Blanc, is located in France, not far from Grenoble. The French Alps are home to many fun activities and sights.

Attractions

Museum of Grenoble

The Museum of Grenoble is the most extensive museum in the city, reveared for its collection of paintings that covers all artistic evolutions. It was the first museum in France to open its collections to modern artists, at the beginning of the 20th century. The collections include paintings by:

  • Rubens
  • Monet
  • Picasso
  • Warhol
  • And many more!

La Bastille

La Bastille is an ancient group of fortifications on the northen side of Grenoble. La Bastille was built in the middle ages and throughout its history has undergone many modifications, such as a semi-underground defense network. In 1934, La Bastille became the destination of the "Grenoble-Bastille Cable Car", known by the locals as "Les Bulles" (the bubbles). Les Bulles provide a wonderous view of the city.
